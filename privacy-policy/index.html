Privacy policy

Privacy policy for BestAgencyCallTracking. What is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a visitor.

Last updated May 27, 2026

This privacy policy describes how BestAgencyCallTracking ("we," "us," or "the site") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit this website.

Information we collect

  • Standard server logs (IP, user agent, referrer) retained for up to 30 days.
  • If analytics is enabled, a privacy-respecting provider that does not set cross-site tracking cookies.
  • Email correspondence retained as needed to respond and follow up.

What we do not collect

No third-party advertising trackers. No Facebook Pixel. No Google Ads remarketing. No data sales.

Cookies

The site does not set marketing or tracking cookies by default. Any cookie used is strictly necessary for site function.

Affiliate links

Affiliate links share a referral ID and a sign-up confirmation with the vendor. We do not receive your personal account data from the vendor.

Your rights

EU, UK, and California residents have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Email editor@bestagencycalltracking.com to exercise them. Response within 30 days.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

Material changes will be flagged at the top of this page for at least 30 days.
